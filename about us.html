ENDING SCHOOL DROPOUT IN AFRICA

  • Ending school dropout in Africa is a community-driven initiative dedicated to ensuring that every child in Africa has access to quality education and the opportunity to complete schoo. We work with students, parents teachers, schools, community leaders and development partners to fight the causes of school dropout and build a brighter future for African children.

    • OUR MISSION:

    • Our mission is to reduce and eventually elimate school drop out across Africa by promoting education awareness, empowering communities, supporting vulnerable learners, and encouraging equal access of education foe all children.

      • OUR VISION:

    • We envision an Africa were every child stays in school, achieves their dreams and contributes positively to the society

    WHAT WE STAND FOR

    • Education for every child

    • Equal opportunities for girls and boys

    • Community empowerment

    • Youth development

    • Hope, dignity and brighter futures
